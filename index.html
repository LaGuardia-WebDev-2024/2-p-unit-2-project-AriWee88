How I make Pizza!!

by Ari

First and most importantly:

The Dough:

For the dough, I use one serving of this recipe to make 2 medium-sized pizzas https://lingeralittle.com/pizza-dough-by-hand/


  • In a cast iron use corn meal or flour to prevent the dough
    from sticking to the bottom.
  • Cut the dough ball in half and roll out both sections to the desired shape and thickness
    (thinner is ideal so you dont get a mouthful of bread).
  • Next, lay it out on the cast iron and adjust the shape if needed, from there, to create the 'crust' allow more dough around the pizza.
    *Use a untensil to differenciate it from the rest.

The Sauce:

  1. I usually get my sibling to make the sauce for me so I'll provide a brief and simple explanation.
  • "Two large spoons of tomato paste, a drizzle of olive oil, and a little bit of water until it reaches the desirable consistency. Add salt to taste and preference.
  • Optional: add oregano or basil

Toppings:

  • Add a layer of mozzarella. Doesn't matter what type. I tend to add more cheese than sauce.

    • Toppings I add to my pizza:
    1. Sausage[cook before adding]
    2. pepperoni
    3. green pepper
    4. olives
    5. mushrooms
    6. sun dried tomatoes(maybe)
  • Arrange the toppings to your liking and then add some mazzarella on top again.

Finally,

- Preheat to 450 - 550 and keep checking it after around 20 mins of cooking until it looks done enough.

ENJOY!
